  • Problem Dataset (Mandatory)

    User must select ONE of the following problem types.

    • Assigned temperatures and pressures - tp
    • Combustion (assigned enthalpy and pressures) - hp
    • Assigned temperatures and either specific volumes or densities - tv
    • Combustion (assigned internal energy and either specific volumes or densities) - uv
    • Rocket - rkt
    • Shock tube - shock
    • Chapman-Jouquet detonation - det
    • Assigned entropy and pressures - sp
    • Assigned entropy and either specific volumes or densities - sv
